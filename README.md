# FlowCredit 💰

*M-Pesa Based Supply Chain Financing Platform*

---

## 🚨 Problem

Small and medium-sized suppliers in Kenya face a major challenge: **limited access to working capital**.

Even when they:

* Have consistent business transactions
* Receive regular payments via M-Pesa
* Work with reliable factories

They still:

* Lack collateral
* Have no formal credit history
* Cannot access timely financing

This leads to:

* Delayed deliveries
* Unstable supply chains
* Missed growth opportunities

---

## 💡 Solution

**FlowCredit** is a digital financing platform that leverages **M-Pesa transaction data** to provide **short-term working capital to suppliers** within a supply chain.

### How it works:

1. Suppliers connect or input their M-Pesa transaction history
2. The system analyzes their cash flow patterns
3. A credit score is generated
4. Suppliers receive financing offers
5. Funds are disbursed via M-Pesa
6. Repayments are automatically deducted from future payments

---

## 📱 About the App

FlowCredit is designed to **embed financial services directly into supply chains**, making financing:

* Faster ⚡
* More accessible 🌍
* Data-driven 📊

The platform connects:

* **Suppliers** → access financing
* **Factories** → ensure stable supply chains
* **Financiers** → reduce lending risk
